What's new in v2.13.0

• New: after an update finishes, this window pops up to briefly tell you what changed (shown in your preferred language — English or 中文).
• New: usage alert — when your current-session usage reaches a threshold you set, a prominent flashing window pops up. Enable "Usage alert" and pick the threshold (60/70/80/90/95%, off by default) in the tray's More menu.
• The tray indicator, usage numbers and notifications all work exactly as before — nothing for you to do.
